Week 5: Enhancements and Advanced Features (July 8 - July 13, 2024)

In Week 5, the focus shifted to implementing advanced features such as real-time chat support, product search, reviews, and implementing an inventory management system for admins to manage stock levels more efficiently.

Day 1: Real-Time Chat Support

Date: Monday, July 8, 2024

Integrated a real-time chat support system to enable customers to communicate directly with customer service representatives. This allows users to ask questions, get support, and resolve issues immediately without leaving the website.

Day 2: Product Search Functionality

Date: Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Developed an advanced product search functionality with filters. Customers can search for products by name, category, price range, and other attributes. The search results are displayed dynamically based on the criteria selected by the user.

Day 3: Product Reviews and Ratings

Date: Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Introduced a product review and rating system, allowing customers to rate and leave reviews for products theyâ€™ve purchased. The reviews are visible to other customers and help in decision-making. Admins can moderate reviews as needed.

Day 4: Inventory Management System

Date: Thursday, July 11, 2024

Implemented an inventory management system for admins to manage stock levels for each product. Admins can view, update, and track inventory for each product, receiving notifications when stock levels are low or out of stock.

Day 5: Order Summary and Invoice Generation

Date: Friday, July 12, 2024

Developed an order summary page for customers to view all their order details including product names, quantities, prices, taxes, shipping fees, and total amount. Additionally, an invoice generation system was added, allowing customers to download PDF invoices for their orders.

Day 6: Admin Dashboard Enhancements

Date: Saturday, July 13, 2024

Improved the admin dashboard by adding analytics and reporting features. Admins can now view reports on sales, popular products, inventory levels, and customer activity. This helps in making data-driven decisions for managing the store more effectively.
