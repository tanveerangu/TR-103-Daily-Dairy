Week 3: Advanced Features and Optimization (June 24 - June 29, 2024)

In Week 3, the focus shifted to improving the performance of the website, adding advanced features such as order tracking, implementing an email notification system, and further optimizing the UI/UX.

Day 1: Order Tracking System

Date: Monday, June 24, 2024

Developed an order tracking system where customers can track the status of their orders. This included integrating the order status in the user dashboard and allowing users to view the progress of their orders (pending, processed, shipped, delivered).

Day 2: Email Notification System

Date: Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Implemented an email notification system using Laravel’s built-in mail functionality. Customers receive notifications for important events such as order confirmation, shipment, and delivery status updates.

Day 3: Product Recommendations (Based on Purchase History)

Date: Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Added a product recommendation feature based on the customer's purchase history. This is displayed on the user's dashboard and product pages, encouraging users to explore additional products they might be interested in.

Day 4: Discount Coupons and Promotions

Date: Thursday, June 27, 2024

Introduced a coupon system to provide discounts and promotions on products. Customers can apply promo codes at checkout to get discounts, and admins can manage the creation and expiration of these coupons.

Day 5: Multi-Language Support

Date: Friday, June 28, 2024

Implemented multi-language support using Laravel’s localization feature. Users can now select their preferred language from a list of available options, enhancing the accessibility of the website for diverse audiences.

Day 6: Performance Optimization

Date: Saturday, June 29, 2024

Focused on improving the website’s performance by optimizing database queries, leveraging Laravel's caching mechanisms, and optimizing images for faster page load times. This ensures a smoother user experience, even with a high number of visitors.
