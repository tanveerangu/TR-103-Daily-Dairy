Week 4: Advanced Functionalities and Security (July 1 - July 6, 2024)

In Week 4, the focus was on adding advanced functionalities such as integrating shipping options, implementing order cancellation, adding customer feedback features, and improving website security.

Day 1: Shipping Options Integration

Date: Monday, July 1, 2024

Integrated various shipping options into the checkout process. Customers can now choose from different shipping methods, each with its own cost and delivery time. Admins can manage the available shipping methods and update them as necessary.

Day 2: Order Cancellation Feature

Date: Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Developed an order cancellation feature where customers can cancel their orders within a specific time frame (e.g., before the order is processed). Admins can review cancellation requests and update the order status accordingly.

Day 3: Customer Feedback System

Date: Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Added a customer feedback system where customers can rate their overall shopping experience and leave suggestions for improvement. Feedback is stored and can be reviewed by the admin to enhance customer satisfaction.

Day 4: Security Enhancements (HTTPS and User Data Protection)

Date: Thursday, July 4, 2024

Focused on improving the website's security by implementing HTTPS to encrypt data transmission. Also added security measures to protect user data, such as password hashing and securing user sessions against unauthorized access.

Day 5: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Date: Friday, July 5, 2024

Implemented two-factor authentication (2FA) to further enhance the security of user accounts. Users can enable 2FA in their profiles, requiring them to enter a code sent to their email or mobile device in addition to their password.

Day 6: Final UI/UX Refinements

Date: Saturday, July 6, 2024

Performed final refinements to the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX). This included improving mobile responsiveness, optimizing button placements, and ensuring a consistent and intuitive design across all pages of the website.
