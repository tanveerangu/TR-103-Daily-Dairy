Week 7: Final Adjustments and Wrap-Up (July 22 - July 24, 2024)

In the final phase of the project, I made final adjustments, fixed any remaining issues, and prepared for a post-launch evaluation. This phase focused on fine-tuning the website's features and ensuring that everything was ready for long-term maintenance.

Date: Monday, July 22, 2024

Date: Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Addressed any remaining bugs or performance issues identified during the post-launch evaluation. Made necessary adjustments to improve user experience, including fine-tuning the search feature, optimizing mobile responsiveness, and enhancing page load speeds.

Day 3: Final Documentation and Project Handoff

Date: Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Completed the final project documentation, including detailed instructions on website maintenance, security best practices, and a roadmap for future updates. Prepared a project handoff to the team or client, ensuring that all necessary files, credentials, and documentation were delivered for long-term support.
